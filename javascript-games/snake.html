How To Play


Controls:

  • W - UP
  • S - DOWN
  • A - LEFT
  • D - RIGHT

(Tip: You can't move the opposite directions. Eg: Up to Down, or Left to Right, or vice versa)


Eat the red dots to get bigger and earn points. If you run into a wall, or run into yourself, you die and it is game over.
