TWO PLAYERS REQUIRED (or just play by yourself)

W = UP
S = DOWN

Right Panel Controls:
UP KEY = UP
DOWN KEY = DOWN

Score by knocking the ball by hitting it into your enemy's "black zone".
First to 5 Points WINS!!!
